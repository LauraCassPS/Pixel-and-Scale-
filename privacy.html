Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 December 2025

At Pixel & Scale, your privacy is important. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or contact us.

1. Information We Collect

Contact details. When you fill out a form or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, website URL (if provided), social links, and any information you choose to share.

Cookies. Our website does not currently use analytics or tracking cookies. If we introduce analytics in future, this policy will be updated to reflect what is collected and why.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide services
  • Improve our website and offerings
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send marketing emails only where you have given consent (you can unsubscribe at any time)

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on:

  • Your consent
  • The performance of a contract or pre-contract steps
  • Compliance with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited data with trusted third-party providers necessary to deliver our services (such as email or analytics platforms). These providers operate under their own privacy policies.

Your data is stored securely and accessed only when necessary.

5. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed by service providers outside your country. Where required, appropriate safeguards are in place to comply with data protection laws such as GDPR.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to or restrict certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact laura@pixelandscale.com.

7. Cookies

Our website does not currently set tracking or advertising cookies. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse or limit cookies at any time. If we add analytics or other cookies in future, we will update this policy.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy or to meet legal requirements.

9. Accessibility

Pixel & Scale is committed to making this website accessible to all users. We aim to follow best practices for clear navigation, readable content, and responsive design.

If you experience any difficulty accessing our site or need content in an alternative format, please contact us at laura@pixelandscale.com.

10. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. The most recent version will always be available on our website.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how your data is handled, please contact laura@pixelandscale.com.
